Who We Are

Parava Arts, Sports & Cultural Club® (Registration No: EKM/TC/274/2024) is a vibrant and committed community organization dedicated to promoting sportsmanship, creativity, and cultural harmony. With active teams in cricket and football, the club strives to nurture youth talent, build confidence, and strengthen unity. Rooted in the heart of Kottappuram, we work passionately to enhance the spirit of community sports and inspire excellence both on and off the field.

PARAVA C C

Parava CC is a dedicated cricket club committed to developing talent, promoting teamwork, and fostering a strong sports culture. With a passion for the game, the club provides continuous training, competitive opportunities, and a supportive environment for players to grow and excel.

PARAVA F C

Parava FC is a passionate football club focused on nurturing talent, strengthening teamwork, and promoting a dynamic sports culture. The club provides continuous training, competitive opportunities, and a supportive environment for players to grow and excel on the field.
